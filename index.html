About Circula

Smart Wastewater IoT for Sustainable Factories

Circula is an internal factory system. We install IoT probes directly into wastewater discharge pipes to measure parameters like pH, temperature, turbidity, and chemical oxygen demand (COD) in real-time. By monitoring the exact condition of production waste before it reaches public waterways (such as rivers), we help factories increase waste processing efficiency, meet regulatory compliance, and drive circular water loops.
